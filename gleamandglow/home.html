If a customer's nail extension gets damaged or breaks within 3 days of receiving the fake nails or nail session, here are some health precautions and possible alternatives or solutions to offer:

Replacement: If the nail extension is significantly damaged, we offer to replace the broken extension with a new one.

To keep nail extensions healthy and long-lasting, customers should follow these key health precautions:

Be Gentle - If the break is minor, we can repair the nail extension using nail adhesive or gel polish.

Moisturize - Use cuticle oil or moisturizing cream to keep the skin around your nails hydrated and healthy.

Keep them Clean - To avoid bacteria accumulation, wash your nail extensions regularly.

Avoid Harsh Chemicals - Steer clear of harsh chemicals, such as bleach or acetone-based solutions, which can damage and lift your nail extensions.

Schedule Regular Maintenance - Keeping your nail extensions in good condition requires regular maintenance.
